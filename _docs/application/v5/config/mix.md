Creating mixes in VectorSurv allows you to record applications using the specific products and chemical combinations your agency utilizes regularly.

![Mix Configuration Form]({{ site.baseurl }}/assets/images/docs/application-v5/mix_form.PNG)

- **Code**: A unique identifier for the mix (e.g., “ULV mix 12”). This field accepts both letters and numbers and should match the naming convention used by your agency.
- **Description**: A brief summary or name for the mix to help staff identify it quickly.
- **Products**: The active ingredients in the mix. Each product entry includes three fields: **Product Name/EPA Number**, **Quantity**, and **Units**.
  - **Adding Products**: Search by name or EPA Registration Number.
  - A mix must contain at least one product.
- **Diluents**: Record any substances used to dilute the active products. Like the products section, this includes three fields for **Name**, **Quantity**, and **Units**.
  - Note: Mixes are not required to include a diluent.

---

> **Important**: Verify the EPA number on your physical product label. Some products may appear multiple times in the system as formulations or registrations change over time.
